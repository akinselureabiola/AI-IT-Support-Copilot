Microsoft 365 Support Guide

Common Issues:
- Teams login failures.
- OneDrive sync issues.
- SharePoint access problems.
- License assignment issues.

Troubleshooting:
- Verify account licensing.
- Verify internet connectivity.
- Sign out and sign back in.
- Clear application cache.
- Verify service health status.

Escalate if:
- Service outage is suspected.
- Tenant-wide issues are reported.
