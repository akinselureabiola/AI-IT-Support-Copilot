VPN Troubleshooting Guide

Error 809:
- Verify internet connectivity.
- Check firewall settings.
- Verify VPN passthrough is enabled on the router.
- Verify UDP ports 500 and 4500 are not blocked.

Error 691:
- Verify username and password.
- Reset credentials if required.
- Check account lockout status.

General VPN Checks:
- Restart VPN client.
- Reboot device.
- Verify VPN service status.
- Test from another network.
- Check for VPN client updates.

Escalate if:
- Multiple users are affected.
- VPN service outage is suspected.

Internal Support Note:

For VPN Error 809, always verify:
- UDP ports 500 and 4500
- VPN passthrough
- Corporate VPN Gateway VPN-BERLIN-01
